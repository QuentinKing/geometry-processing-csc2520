  [Jacobson et al. 2013]

  [Bergou et al. 2008]

  "Screened Poisson Surface Reconstruction" [Kazhdan & Hoppe 2013]
  "Parallel Poisson Surface Reconstruction" [Bolitho et al. 2009]
  "Poisson Surface Reconstruction" [Kazhdan et al. 2006]
